Kill Gil(bert)

In this game the player is tasked with clearing a room full of enemies called 'Gilbert' (I hoped that humanizing them would add an extra layer of difficulty to the game). Eliminating all the Gilberts in a room allows the player to advance to the next room with stronger, faster, and unique Gilberts. Killing an enemy will award the player with coins that they can use to buy upgrades in the shop. These upgrades will allow the player to get father and surpass their high score!
Controls: WASD to move, mouse to aim, click to shoot, 'R' to reload.

Kill Gil Arena Brawl

This game relies on similar mechanics to the original Kill Gil(bert) however this time the enemies are other players! Players who are playing at the same time will be able to fight each other to the death! Move around the arena launching fireballs at your oponent. Be careful though, the obstacles deflect the fireballs and its very easy to end up hitting yourself.
Controls: WASD to move, mouse to aim, click to shoot.

Boop the Stroop
