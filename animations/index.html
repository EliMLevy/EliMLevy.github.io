Animations

In April of 2020, locked down because of Corona Virus, I attempted to learn a 3D animation software called Blender. There is a wealth of Blender tutorials avaliable on YouTube which made this project really fun and productive. After months of following tutorials and reading documentation I found that my lack of artistic ability made high level 3D modeling really difficult. So I didn't continue to pursue this skill after that.
The bedtime story animation was the most complicated scene I ever made. Low-poly modeling poeple took a lot of trial and error to get a result that moderately resembled a human.
The Jet fighter and Canyon animation is rather simple, but uses a particle system and real topological data from the grand canyon (from this website).
The Rocketship Experiment animation was an early creation where I modeled the rocketship after an image i found on the internet.
Lastly, Glow Ball is a very simple animation but it uses a technique called drivers to change the hue of the object based on its position.
I hope you enjoy them!
